Chandan Tuskers

Chandan Tuskers, also known as CT, is the Goa based cricket franchise. The team is owned by Rohan Naik Gaonkar , a Business Magnate. CT has won many tournaments in last five-six years. The team had 3 good seasons back-to-back when they made it to the final in 2019. Led by Aditya Gaonkar, CT yet again finished 2nd in 2020. RCB has adopted the philosophy #PlayBold which resonates both on and off the field - ‘Defeats don’t defeat us, the Challenger Spirit keeps us alive’
